Privacy Policy Authoring Rules
===============================

1. Do not mention marketplace brand names (e.g., “Apple App Store”, “Google Play”, “Play Store”, “App Store”). Use generic phrasing such as “the distribution platform” or “your platform’s marketplace”.
2. Disclose third-party SDKs and services by their product/vendor names (e.g., Firebase) and link to their privacy documentation where helpful.
3. Describe what data is collected, why it is collected, and who processes it. Avoid claiming “no data collected” if analytics, crash reporting, or performance monitoring are enabled.
4. State user choices/opt-out paths if available; if not available, say so plainly.
5. Keep the “Last updated” date accurate whenever the policy text changes.
6. Use simple, direct language; avoid legalese unless required by the app’s distribution platforms or jurisdictions.
