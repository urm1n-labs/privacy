Image Resizer ("we", "our", or "us") provides a mobile application that allows users to select, compress, preview, and save images on their devices.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when it may be shared, and what choices you have when using the app.

Your images never leave your device as part of the core resizing and compression flow. Image processing happens locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to operate the app, process purchases, support advertising for free users, and improve reliability.

A. Images and Media You Select

When you choose images from your device:

  • the app accesses only the images you select or allow us to access
  • selected images may be temporarily processed or cached on your device
  • compressed images may be saved back to your device gallery if you choose to save them

We do not sell your images.

We do not intentionally upload your selected images to our servers for image compression as part of the core image optimization flow.

B. Purchase and Subscription Information

If you purchase premium access, we use RevenueCat and the relevant distribution platform provider to manage purchases, entitlements, restore purchases, and related transaction status.

We may receive information such as:

  • purchase status
  • entitlement status
  • anonymous app user identifier or purchaser identifier assigned by RevenueCat or the platform

  • transaction metadata made available to us through RevenueCat

We do not receive your full payment card details. Payments are processed by the applicable platform marketplace.

C. Advertising Data

If you use the free version of the app, we may display ads through Google AdMob or other advertising partners we may add in the future.

Advertising providers may collect or receive information such as:

  • device identifiers
  • advertising identifiers
  • approximate device and network information
  • ad interactions, impressions, clicks, and reward events
  • diagnostic and performance information

This information may be used by the advertising provider to serve, measure, personalize, or limit advertising as permitted by applicable law and platform rules.

D. Device, Diagnostics, and Usage Data

We may collect limited technical information to keep the app functioning and to diagnose crashes or errors, including:

  • app version

  • operating system version
  • crash logs
  • performance data
  • basic app event data related to purchases, ads, or image processing errors

E. Permissions

Depending on your device and how you use the app, we may request access to:

We request permissions only when needed for app features.

Image processing is intended to occur locally on your device as part of the app's core optimization workflow.

Temporary files may be created on your device during processing, previewing, exporting, or saving. These files may remain in device or app cache storage until removed by the app, the operating system, or by uninstalling the app.

You are responsible for reviewing images before saving, sharing, or deleting them.
